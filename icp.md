# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Definition

Our ideal customer is a games studio that develops mobile games. They can also develop console / PC games, but they must develop at least one mobile game. These studios  design, build, and publish mobile games for platforms such as iOS and Android. They may be independent and typically have a dedicated team focused on game development, design, art, and user acquisition. They generate revenue through in-app purchases, ads, or paid downloads.

Key signals to look for:
- Develops and publishes mobile games (for iOS and/or Android)
- Identifies as a games studio or game developer
- Portfolio of released or upcoming mobile game titles
- Presence on app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play, etc.)
- Focus on the gaming industry, not general software or enterprise apps
